At BGS, we are passionate about increasing biodiversity; it’s at the very heart of why Will formed the company.

If you have an area of the garden that you are unsure on what to do with – Wildflower turf can be a great solution. Not only is it fantastic for the environment increasing biodiversity & attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies it is also a low maintenance solution requiring only one-two cuts per year!

Did you know that wildflowers and grasses help to catch both water & air pollution. They actually capture co2 more efficiently than trees and store it into the soil. There are multiple choices of wildflower products with six pre-made turf options: Landscape 34 Turf, Species Rich Lawn Turf, Shade Tolerant, SuDS, Native Enriched, Wildflower Roof Turf.

If the job is undertaken in the autumn additional interest and variety can be added by spreading a crate of specially selected bulbs underneath the turf. Another way in which we can create a wildflower area is to spread Meadowscape Pro which is a premixed seed and topsoil.

Bespoke turf and Meadowscape can also be created and installed upon request.
